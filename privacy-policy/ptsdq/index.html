PTSDQ — Privacy Policy

Developer: Yeminizz / Q Series
Last updated: March 25, 2026

PTSDQ is designed for PTSD screening through the PC-PTSD-5 and PCL-5 questionnaires and PPG analysis. This Privacy Policy describes how the app handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All measurement data, including survey responses and sensor readings, is stored locally on your device and remains under your control.

2. Camera Usage

PTSDQ uses the device camera solely for PPG heart rate measurement. Camera data is processed on-device in real-time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any external server.

3. Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for the purpose of serving personalized advertisements. You can opt out of personalized advertising through the App Tracking Transparency prompt displayed at first launch, or through your device settings at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — advertising display and ad personalization

No other third-party services receive your data. All health and sensor data remains on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
